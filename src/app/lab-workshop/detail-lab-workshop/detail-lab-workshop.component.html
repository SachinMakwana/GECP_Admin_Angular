NAME OF LAB AND WORKSHOP:
Mechanical Workshop
CATEGORY:
Workshop
DEPARTMENT:
Mechanical
Detail Description
Dynamic loads and undesired oscillations increase with higher speed of machines. At that time, knowledge and practical exposure is required for better vibration reduction. The Laboratory deals with balancing of mechanisms, torsional and bending vibrations, vibration isolation, and the dynamic behavior of drives and machine frames as complex systems using static and dynamic balancing apparatus, whirling of shaft apparatus and universal vibration apparatus. Main objective of study about dynamics is to understand how a static and dynamic mass is balanced, To study about different types of vibration.
